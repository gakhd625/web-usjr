SCHOOLS

The University is made up of twelve degree-granting, academic schools. While each school has its own personality and area of expertise, they frequently work together to offer interdisciplinary degree programs and undertake complex research.

School of Allied Medical Sciences

The School of Allied Medical Sciences has adopted an Open Admission, Strict Retention and Promotion Policy in order to inspire the clientele from the Visayas and Mindanao regions. It is committed to produce globally competent nurses who shall make a big difference in the Nursing Profession.

School of Arts and Sciences
The School of Arts and Sciences' prime goal is the maximum development of the students toward self-realization and observance of ethical and moral standards of behaviour within the framework of Christian living.
School of Business and Management
The degree programs of the School of Business and Management endeavor to produce professionally competent graduates who exemplify moral integrity and willingness to serve the society.
School of Computer Studies
The School of Computer Studies unified all the computed-related programs under one umbrella and address the demands of the global market which requires more agile and need-based curriculums.
School of Education
The School of Education aims to develop Christian, community-oriented, competent and research-based aspiring teachers, thoroughly trained and equipped for academic and professional responsibilities in the elementary and secondary levels of education.
School of Engineering
The School of Engineering of the University of San Jose Recoletos, since its start of operation, always embark on various activities geared to strengthen the college in technological and in the spiritual aspects relevant to the needs of time. Serving as guide for the different activities to be conducted, are the goals and objectives of the school.
School of Law
The primary goal of the College of Law is the preparation and development of good Christian and socially-oriented lawyers by providing a comprehensive legal education program through proper instruction and guidance on the principles of law and the system of administration of justice in the country and abroad.
ETEEAP
The ETEEAP is a comprehensive educational assessment program at the tertiary level that recognizes, accredits and gives equivalencies to knowledge, skills, attitudes and values gained by individuals from relevant work.
RITTC
The center intends to provide cost-effective technical-vocational training and serves as an avenue of endless opportunities. It is geared towards the development and optimization of the individual's full potential through knowledge and skills training. It caters them to be productive and resilient citizens in a competitive global society.
